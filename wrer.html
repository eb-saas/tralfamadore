LET GO OF WHAT YOU KNOW ; WRITE IN PERMENAT INK ; DESTROY IT WHEN DONE AND DO IT AGAIN ; NOW ; KILL IT ALL

[ER:SC]

they all write in premanent ink
& when they are done writing
they destroy it all & write it again

they all draw in permment ink
& when they are done drawing
they destroy it & draw it again

the words aren't good & the
drawings are worse. the writing
& drawing is beautiful, though

they make many mistakes;
except they were’nt.

Explorer’s note: These creatures appear to have no set form. They constantly shift forms, except for their eyes, which remain the same no matter which form they take. I am not sure as to what they call themselves, as every time I ask a different one I get a different answer. I have taken to calling them the “Whateverers” for their apparent lack of care in defining themselves. I hope this decision I made will not haunt them in later years.
